ScotsWaterSports - Surfing Page

surfing page

Surfing is one of the most addictive, adrenaline fuelled sports on the planet; Surrounded by Atlantic and North Sea Swells, you will always be guaranteed to catch some great waves in scotland; Along with sandy beaches, long coastlines and beautiful clear waves this makes Scotland one of Europe's top surfing destinations.

Here are a few suggestions for Surfing in Scotland
  • Thurso - With waves known to range from 2 to
    15 feet, this is the ideal place for thrill seekers
    and serious wave riders
  • Pease Bay - Located in the Scottish borders,
    this area offers some dynamite reef for pros
    and a decent beach break for all skill levels
  • Belhaven Bay - a smooth, flat beach and lack of
    rocks makes this a great location for surfing newbies
